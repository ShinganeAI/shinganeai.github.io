Welcome to Shingane AI

Shingane AI is a pioneering physical AI company dedicated to bridging the gap between artificial intelligence and the real world. Our mission is to create intelligent systems that seamlessly interact with physical environments, empowering industries to achieve new levels of automation, efficiency, and innovation.

From advanced robotics to smart infrastructure, we design and deploy AI-driven solutions that transform how people and machines collaborate. Our team is passionate about pushing the boundaries of what AI can achieve in the physical domain.

Our Company Culture

At Shingane AI, we foster a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and continuous learning. We believe that diverse perspectives drive innovation, and we encourage every team member to contribute ideas and challenge the status quo.

Integrity, transparency, and respect are at the core of our values. We are committed to building a supportive environment where everyone can thrive and make a meaningful impact on the future of AI.
